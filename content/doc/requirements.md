<p>To build on Ubuntu you will need the following packages</p>

* pilot-link
* libpisock-dev
* libpisock9
* autopoint
* autoconf
* intltool
* libtool
* libgcrypt20-dev
* libgcrypt20

For J-Pilot versions 1.x you will need:
* libgtk2.0-dev

Likewise, for J-Pilot 2.x you need
* libgtk3.0-cil-dev
